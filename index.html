OpenLiving Cell Analysis



OpenLiving Cell Analysis is a highly ambitious project. In last 17 months month, we have developed several tasks to achieve the objectives of the project. Initially, we found difficulties regarding data collection in a India specific scenario. We found there are many difficulties such as the law, regulations, availability of experts in India for data collection and Imaging, easy access of Imaging devices (Microscopy), etc. are the main hurdle of the project. We prepare a report on these challenges.


The broad objectives of OpenLivingCellAnalysis are:


1. Create a ground-breaking research platform that is open-source, aligned with Indian perspective, and will contribute towards state-of-the-art live-cell microscopy analysis combining multiple discipline and will influence global research in this area.
2. Redefine the possibilities for live-cell video analysis research and its impact on finding new age solutions for diseases and supporting new knowledge discovery.
3. Create a lasting impact on global live-cell microscopy analysis.
4. Build a strong consortium of young research leaders.


The scientific objectives of OpenLivingCellAnalysis are:


1. To develop AI powered open-source tools for live-cell fluorescence and label-free video analysis.
2. Design new metrics, novel analytics that will help new knowledge discovery from live-cell microscopy data.
3. Develop state-of-the-art AI based methods for microscopy video analysis as easy configurable, interpretable, and open-source module for multiple domain of researchers such as AI researchers, biologists, imaging persons, doctors, etc.
4. Provide a common platform to collaborate, develop, interpret live-cell microscopy data among Indian and global scientists.
